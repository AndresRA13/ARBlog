Family Member Presentation

Introducing a loved one using basic English expressions

In this video activity, I presented a family member using simple sentences. I described their name, age, birthday, nationality, hobbies, and daily activities. It helped me practice vocabulary and structures related to personal information and everyday routines.

This task improved my confidence in speaking English and using visual aids. It is essential for professional contexts where personal introductions and clear communication are important.

My Daily Routine

Audio description of my academic and work habits

I recorded an audio in which I described my academic and professional responsibilities using modal verbs such as must, have to, and should. I reflected on the importance of discipline, punctuality, and ethical values in daily activities.

This helped me express ideas clearly and responsibly. Learning to talk about responsibilities is key to workplace communication and self-management.
